Frequently Asked Questions

How much do your services cost?

Attorney fees are calculated on an individual basis and depend on the complexity of the issue, the category of dispute, the court instance, location, etc. For an exact amount, please contact us using the contact information provided on the website. With us, the client is always informed in advance about attorney fees and costs.

What are your working hours?

Our main working hours are Mon-Fri 10:00-18:00. However, our hotline number works 24/7 to provide emergency assistance to clients in criminal cases when needed.

How to contact you?

All the information to contact us is indicated on the website. By clicking on the "Contact" button, you will see our contact information. For emergency assistance during night hours, use our hotline number or WhatsApp application, where you can write about your problem, and our team member will respond immediately.

What is the consultation fee and how do I receive it?

For a consultation, you need to contact us. The consultation is free for the first 10 minutes. The following price depends on the complexity of the issue and is calculated hourly. The price of a telephone consultation for one hour is 250 GEL. The price of a written consultation starts from 350 GEL. And a consultation with a lawyer in person is calculated hourly from 450 GEL. The consultation is free in all cases when the client receives legal services.
